ABOUT OLEOPRENS

A new series of Artlife’s biologically active complexes is made on the basis of studies of the Russian scientists, who discovered the unique properties of Abies spectabilis. This component that is obtained as a result of a technologically complicated multi step extraction of fir, plays an important role in the energy metabolism and cellular protection, restores body’s vital resources and speeds up regeneration processes.
